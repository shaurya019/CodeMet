  • What is CodeChef?

    Codechef organizes mainly 3 types of official challenges per month -

    1.Codechef Long Challenge : A challenge that goes on from the first Friday to the next Monday of ever month. So, there are 10 days to solve 8 problems of varying difficulty.

    2. CodeChef Lunchtime : The contest is of 3 hours duration and consists of 45 problems. This enables problem solving skills in a time constraint.

    3. CodeChef Cook-off : A 2.5 hour contest organized every month that consists of mostly medium to hard questions that enables deep and fast thinking.

  • What is CodeForces?

    The Codeforces platform is typically used when preparing for competitive programming contests and it offers the following features:

    1.Short (2-hours) contests, called "Codeforces Rounds", held about once a week

    2.Educational contests (2-2.5 hours, with 24 hours hacking period), held 2-3 times per month;

    3.Challenge/hack other contestants' solutions;

    4.Solve problems from previous contests for training purposes;

    5."Polygon" feature for creating and testing problems;
